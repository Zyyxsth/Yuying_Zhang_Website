This work presents a real-time UAV dodging framework that uses only an onboard RGB-D camera. Human pose and depth cues enable projectile trajectory prediction before release, while an uncertainty-aware strategy supports timely and robust evasive planning.

The perception system achieves a 6 m effective detection range with 26.4 ms average latency on CPU-only hardware. Real-world flight experiments demonstrate a 96.67% overall dodging success rate across diverse attack scenarios.

**Yuying Zhang is a co-first author and the first-listed author.** The paper was transferred to ICRA 2026.

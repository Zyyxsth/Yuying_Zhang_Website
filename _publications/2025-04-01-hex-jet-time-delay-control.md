This work introduces the Hex-Jet, a turbojet-powered VTOL UAV that combines thrust vectoring and differential thrust for attitude control. Predictor-based time-delay control is used to mitigate the slow turbojet dynamics, with successful validation through comparative studies and flight experiments.

**Yuying Zhang is the third author.**
